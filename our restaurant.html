DAPUR DIBU






Our Journey

Due to the Covid-19 outbreak that rocked our nation in 2019, the dibu cooking stall's history began. It is now the lone location, and all customer orders are only accepted online. Since there are always ups and downs in business, Dibu's kitchen had to temporarily close in 2020 as a result of its financial difficulties. Following that, in 2021, Dibu's Kitchen flourished once more by accepting online orders once more and providing COD (cash on delivery) deliveries. Following that, in 2021, the orders continue to increase and both our regular clients and new customers are enthusiastic about our company. After we had successfully piqued the interest of our clients, we began walking up to a coffee shop. After visiting people's stores for a year, we decided not to visit the cafe again, so in 2023 we rose up by opening a dibu cooking warung in Jenderam Hilir Dengkil, a neighbourhood near Shell Jenderam Hilir.
