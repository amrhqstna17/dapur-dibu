Dapur Dibu



Our only premises - from our central kitchen to all branches - are Halal certified. We also practice strict internal control from the source of raw materials to its preparation and presentation to our customers.

Strict hygiene practices are applied in food preparation as well as our staff. Cleanliness and Halal compliance checks are carried out regularly at all our branches.

Our Halal Internal Audit team and Halal committee also regularly conduct surprise audit inspections at our own premises as well as our suppliers' premises to ensure our stores and suppliers comply with the high standards of Halal certification.
